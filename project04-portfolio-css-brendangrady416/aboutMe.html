Get to Know Me

Explore more about my background, interests, and skills.

I am currently an undergraduate student at the University of Denver, pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science as well as minors in Mathematics and Business Administration. I have taken classes in Object-Oriented Programming, Data Structures, Web Programming, Computer Systems, and Discrete Mathematics. I am proficient in Java, Python, and HTML/CSS.

I am originally from Holden, Massachusetts, and have been living in Colorado for the past few years for school. I am a big fan of Boston sports, which have been pretty nice in my lifetime. Last Fall, I studied abroad in Cork, Ireland, and was able to travel all over Europe. At the University of Denver, I am treasurer of the Club Kayaking Team. Back home in Massachusetts, I have a yellow lab named Penny.
